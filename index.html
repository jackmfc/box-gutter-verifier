How the curvature check works
Water flowing around a bend banks up on the outer wall (superelevation): Δh = V²·b/(g·R). Half this rise is added to the design water level at the outer wall; the check confirms freeboard there still meets the minimum, so the gutter does not overtop into the building — addressing the 1% AEP "must not enter the building" criterion.
This tool is a calculation aid supporting a Performance Solution. It assesses the plan-curvature departure and the overflow condition; it assumes the straight box gutter is otherwise sized to AS/NZS 3500.3. Clause references are reproduced from the NCC for the selected edition and must be confirmed by the responsible practitioner against the current, in-force NCC and any state variations. Outputs do not constitute regulatory approval.
